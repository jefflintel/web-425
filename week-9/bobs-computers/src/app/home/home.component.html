A little history

Bob and his talented staff of computer repair technicians have been tinkering with computers since the very beginning. Bob was a summer intern with Charles Babbage, and later went on to work with Alan Turing. He's familiar with all types of computer systems, from mechanical calculating machines all the way up to the latest and greatest technology.

Bob scoured the world for the best technicians to hire. He sought people who were at the top of their field and recruited them to work in his Bellevue, Nebraska shop. He has managed to amass one of the greatest collections of repair talent anywhere in the world.
